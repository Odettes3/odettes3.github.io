Odette Surtees

About

Odette Surtees is an emerging writer. She is interested in writing in a combination of styles and theories.

Odette tries not to condense herself into one specific category as she is always looking to adapt and manipulate her writing in unexpected and unusual ways.

Her latest project is questioning what it means to combine literature with the visual arts.

Styles:

  • Prose
  • Short-Scripts
  • Poetry
  • Electronic Literature

Areas of Interest:

  • Postmodernism
  • Psychoanalytic Theory
  • Eco-Criticism
  • Literature and Calligraphy
